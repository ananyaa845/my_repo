About Us

CozyCrumbs has been serving its customers since 2019. Here's about our journey:

Our journey

  1. We started out as a small bakery run from home with few items.
  2. After gaining some popularity, we opened our first store near our home which quickly became a hit spot.
  3. Started Online ordering and Delivery services.
  4. Opened two more branches in different locations in the city.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring warmth, comfort, and joy to every home through our fresh, homemade baked goods.
We believe in using quality ingredients and handcrafted recipes that make every bite feel like home.

Our Vision

We dream of becoming the go-to bakery in the city and beyond where every celebration, big or small, includes a little bit of Cozy Crumbs.
Our vision is to spread happiness, one crumb at a time!
