OUR SERVICES:

We offer freshly baked goods daily across all our branches.
Apart from it, we provides services like:

  • Custom cakes for birthdays, weddings, or any occasions.
  • Catering for any special events.
  • Online Ordering (the order must be made atleast a day prior delivery).

    • Custom Cake Order Process:

    1. Choose your cake design, flavor, size, and preferred decoration e.g. fondant or cream-based.
    2. Place your order in-store, by phone, or through our website.
    3. We confirm the order details including price, delivery options, and lead time.
    4. Pick up your cake at the store or get it delivered fresh to your doorstep.

    Technical Terms:

    1. Fondant
      2. A smooth, moldable icing used to give cakes a clean and elegant finish, often used for special occasion cakes.
    2. Lead Time
      3. The amount of time required between placing an order and receiving the finished product.
